About me

I hail from and spent the entirety of my formative years in the NW Iowa farm town of Spencer. Known to many as the jewel of Northwestern Iowa Spencer is acclaimed far and wide for its supremacy in farmland, open spaces, friendly people, wind power generation, and as a pipeline of management information systems talent to The University of Iowa. As a youngster I flourished as an integral member of Spencer's high school football team. My job was to lure an unsuspecting defensive back away from the center of the field and towards the sideline. Once I accomplished this feat I would first confirm with the referee that I was indeed onsides and then I would proceed to block the surprised defender as the running back ran the ball to the left or right of the center. I spent eighteen years and two months in the green pastures of Spencer before voluntarily relocating to Iowa City Iowa to enroll in The University of Iowa. The University of Iowa, which of course I'm sure you are all aware, is known for its sobriety, chastity, and its numerous men's volleyball championships. I was right at home. After several days of attending classes at Iowa I blossomed into a stellar student and became the man that I am today.
